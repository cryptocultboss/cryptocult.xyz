Crypto Cult Is A Community For Holders

Not everyone is here for the same reason.

Some are here for alpha.

Some are just flipping and moving on.

And some are looking to hold alongside people they trust.

Crypto Cult is a Solana-based crypto community built around the $CULT token, created for that last group.

It's for people drawn together by shared belief, culture, and long-term participation. For those who want to build alongside other holders they respect. Who value conversation over shouting. Who know real progress happens when people feel seen.

This isn't about chasing the next move.
It's about recognizing each other.
